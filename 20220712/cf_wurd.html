*** guess the correct word ***


when you submit your guess, the hex will show you clues

the order of the clues doesn't relate to the order of your guess letters


-- an example --


if you type in 'dental' as your guess, the result may look like this


d e n t a l

-- how to read the clues --

- letters on green backgrounds -

you correctly guessed two letters they are in the right place and form part of the secret wurd

- border triangles with numbers -

each number means a letter in this guess is correct but needs to move # spaces

In this case one letter is 3 away the other is 5

- border triangles with an X -

each one of these means that one of the guess letters is incorrect

d e n t a l

--- what this all means ---

_ _ n t _ _

- we know these n and t are correct from the solid background.

- there is a letter 5 away from where it should be

- there is a letter 3 away from where it should be

- there are 2 letters that we don't know much about


develop strategies as you play to solve in fewer guesses. if you don't have a clue, just guess another word!

m i n t e d

with this guess 4 letters are known

_ _ n t e d

only a few words could fit...

h u n t e d

solved :)


--- tips ---


* long-press on the keyboard to mark a letter as ruled out


* the word never contains multiples of the same letter


* if you know a letter, you can insert it into your guess to hold its place


* the word is never plural


* there is a new wurd every day
